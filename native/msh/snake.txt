Welcome to the game!
Snake.

In the game, YOU are a snake to pick up food, try to avoid hitting 
your own tail or the "walls" that surround the playing area.

Each time the snake eats a piece of food, its tail grows longer! 
Making the game increasingly difficult!

How to Control?


   W                    UP

A  +  D         LEFT    +    RIGHT

   S                   DOWN

You can press + and - to adjust the speed of the snake.

Press + to Make It FASTER!
Press - to Make It SLOWER!

Press Q to QUIT THE GAME.

ENJOY IT!!!
